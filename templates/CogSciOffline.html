What is Cognitive Science at F&M?

Click a button to see details.

Cognitive Science at F&M is an interdisciplinary major that explores the mind through multiple perspectives: psychology, neuroscience, computer science, philosophy, and linguistics. Students investigate how we think, learn, perceive, and use language, drawing on scientific methods and computational models to understand cognition.

  • Interdisciplinary approach combining psychology, neuroscience, computer science, philosophy, and linguistics.
  • Hands-on research opportunities in cognitive labs and computational modeling.
  • Explore artificial intelligence, language processing, perception, and decision-making.
